About Us

Making of the system

Railway Guider founded to help startups, and it still shapes the way we work today During each of the periods here is our process,

  1. Purpose to starting of the project.

    08 April 2020

    The main purpose for making this app is to find better solution for train travelers.

  2. Why we select this path as an app

    17 May 2020

    When we finding the problems of people due various streams we found a problem due to SL railway. Then we start to make an app as a solution for this problem.

  3. The fund for this process

    26 June 2020

    Google is the main source we found information to our process. Further studying about train schedules we use algorithm. YouTube, tracking system books are the other sources we use to get information.

  4. Launch our website

    03 July 2020

    We are going to launch this application in 03rd July 2020. We are releasing only the demo at that day. In the future as soon as we possible we will release the original system.
